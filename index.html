Ancient Warriors (AW) is a collection of 4,444 warriors hailing from some of the greatest African and Latin American empires in history. We are building a decentralized empire that represents the might of the Aztecs, Zulus, Inkas, and Benin Kingdoms.

The AW team has been public and transparent since the beginning of the project. The founders studied at Princeton University & MIT and built venture-backed tech companies in Nigeria and Ecuador. Our team is entirely of African and Latin-American descent.

Our 3 Artists fused a blend of Afro and Latin American styles to design 200+ traits, each of them unique to a specific culture and gender combination. From there, each NFT is programmatically generated.

We plan to launch our presale for Legion Warriors in mid-November (date TBA). The public sale will come shortly after with a minting fee of 0.05 eth.
